📖 The Word in Context - How to Use

Hands-Free Mode (Wake Word "John")

  • Toggle the HF switch in the input bar to enable hands-free.
  • Say "John" (or your custom wake word) followed by your question, e.g. "John tell me about John 3:16".
  • It listens continuously, uses wake word to start, and you can say the wake word again to barge-in while it's speaking.
  • For long pauses, say "[John] pause" or "hold".
  • The live transcript shows improved Bible reference recognition.

Voices (Text-to-Speech)

  • Click 🔊 Voice to open settings.
  • Choose from your device's system voices (all available, including premium/enhanced on Mac/iPhone).
  • Use 🔊 test buttons to preview any voice with John 3:16.
  • Speed and pitch sliders adjust the voice.
  • On iPhone: tap "🔊 Speak test (unlock iPhone voices)" or any preview to force download premium voices to appear.
  • Reload system voices after installing new ones like Piper.
  • Auto-speak can be toggled for AI replies.
Tip: Numbered internal voices on Mac are real - use the 🔊 buttons to discover if they sound male or female.

Sources & Citations

  • Click 📚 Sources to see live citations from bible.helloao.org.
  • Every scripture reference in replies is grounded with exact text from original languages (Greek SBL, Hebrew WLC) and literal English translations.
  • Click sources in replies to expand details and speak the verses.

Sidebar & Chats

  • Click the book icon or hamburger to open sidebar with chat history (Grok-like multi-conversation).
  • Search chats, create new, or switch between conversations.
  • Chats are saved in your browser only.

Mobile Tips

  • Input bar stays fixed just above the keyboard on focus.
  • HF toggle is visible compact next to send button.
  • Larger tappable speak buttons on replies.
  • Full scrolling, no cutoff, thumb-friendly 44px targets.
