Why Word in Context is different

🗣️ "Holy Grok" Hands-Free

Say the wake word and have natural conversations. Long pauses supported. It only listens when addressed. Perfect for driving, walking, or study time.

📜 Live Greek & Hebrew Sources

Every scripture reference pulls live from the public API. Greek NT (SBLGNT, Byzantine, TR) and Hebrew OT (Westminster Leningrad Codex) shown and spoken with transliteration.

🔊 Free High-Quality Voices

All your Mac system voices (including paid packs) work perfectly — unlimited and free. Optional ElevenLabs with your own key (you pay them directly).

💬 Grok-like Conversations

Full history sidebar (Today / Yesterday / Older), rename, delete, search. Persistent across refreshes. Exactly like using Grok but grounded in Scripture.

✅ Verifiable Citations

Every quote includes exact source + translation. Click Sources under any reply to see the live-fetched text. Speak the exact verses with one tap.

🔒 Private & Low Cost

Your chats stay on your device. Server only proxies Grok (your key never exposed). Designed for sustainable low monthly subscription ($4–7 range).
